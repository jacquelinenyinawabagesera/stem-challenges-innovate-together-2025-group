Weekend Babysitting

Sister's Salary

Hourly rate: 12 GHS

Total hours worked: 48 hours (weekend)

Total earnings: 48 × 12 = 576 GHS

Brother's Payment

Saturday night coverage from 9 p.m. to 3 a.m. = 6 hours

Payment to brother = 10% of total weekend salary

10% × 576 = 57.6 GHS

Amount sister keeps after paying brother:

576 - 57.6 = 518.4 GHS
