Survival Slide

An Evolutionary Board Game on Natural Selection

Game Overview

Compete as Lizards or Fish in a race for survival!

Flick your animal discs through the center gate to win.

Learn how traits and adaptation shape evolution.

Understand how environments select for survival traits.

How to Play

  • Set up the board with 5 animal discs per player (lizards vs. fish).
  • Each player chooses a species and places their discs on their side.
  • On “GO”, use one finger to flick discs through the center gate.
  • Discs must pass through the gate only—no pushing or throwing!
  • First to get all 5 discs through wins the round.

Animal Adaptations

Trait/Mutation Helps Who? Outcome
Fast runners Cheetahs, rabbits Catch or escape prey
Camouflage Moths, lizards Avoid predators
Strong beaks Finches Break hard seeds
Heat resistance Desert animals Survive climate

Evolution in Action

  • Superbugs: Bacteria are evolving resistance to antibiotics.
  • Urban wildlife: Animals adapt to city noise and light.
  • Diet changes: Some birds now prefer fast food scraps over seeds!

Evolution never stops—nature keeps selecting what works best!
